About Prince George Transport

Based at 200 Louthian Way, Blythewood, SC 29016, Prince George Transport is a licensed land ambulance provider specializing in non-emergency medical transport. Our EMT-trained team coordinates with medical offices, dialysis centers, and families to ensure every patient arrives safely and on schedule.

We also sell used ambulance supplies and two-way radios — stretchers, oxygen equipment, monitoring gear, and Motorola/Kenwood radios retired from our fleet. Great for volunteer departments, training programs, and budget-conscious agencies.
